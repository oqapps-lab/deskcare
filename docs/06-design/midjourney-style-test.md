# DeskCare — Тест стилистики для упражнений (Midjourney)

## Упражнение для теста: Наклон головы влево (Neck Side Bend)

Сгенерируй все 5 вариантов и выбери стиль, который нравится.

---

## Вариант 1: Soft 3D (как Headspace)

> A soft 3D rendered illustration of a young woman sitting at a modern office desk, gently tilting her head to the left in a neck stretch. Calm expression, eyes closed. Soft pastel mint and lavender color palette. Rounded smooth 3D shapes, minimal details. Clean light gray background. Warm ambient lighting. Wellness app aesthetic similar to Headspace or Calm. Side view, waist up. --ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --style raw

---

## Вариант 2: Flat Illustration (как Notion/Linear)

> Flat vector illustration of a woman doing a neck stretch at her desk, head tilted left. Modern minimal flat design, no gradients, bold shapes. Warm coral and teal color palette on white background. Clean geometric style. Simple office desk with laptop. Professional and clean aesthetic. Side view, waist up. --ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --style raw

---

## Вариант 3: Warm Lifestyle (фото-реалистичный стиль)

> Photorealistic lifestyle illustration of a young professional woman at a minimal Scandinavian desk, doing a gentle neck stretch tilting head to left. Soft natural window light, warm tones. Cozy home office setting. Shallow depth of field. Peaceful, mindful moment. Shot from slightly above, waist up. --ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --style raw

---

## Вариант 4: Anime/Manga Wellness

> Anime-style illustration of a cute young woman at an office desk stretching her neck to the left. Soft cel shading, big expressive eyes closed peacefully. Light pink and sky blue palette. Clean white desk with small plant. Kawaii wellness aesthetic. Gentle glow effects. Side view, waist up. --ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --style raw

---

## Вариант 5: Paper Cut / Craft

> Paper cut art style illustration of a woman stretching her neck at a desk. Layered paper effect with subtle shadows between layers. Warm earth tones: terracotta, sage green, cream. Textured paper background. Handcrafted organic feel. Modern craft aesthetic. Side view, waist up. --ar 3:4 --v 6.1 --style raw

---

## После выбора стиля

Когда выберешь один из 5 — используй `--sref` с seed выбранной картинки для всех остальных упражнений. Это сохранит единый стиль.

## Пайплайн дальше

1. Генерируешь 30 картинок (одна на упражнение) в выбранном стиле
2. Скачиваешь 30 коротких референс-клипов с YouTube/Mayo Clinic
3. Загружаешь пару (картинка + референс) в Kling 3.0
4. Получаешь 30 анимированных клипов по 5 сек
5. Зацикливаешь и кладёшь в приложение
